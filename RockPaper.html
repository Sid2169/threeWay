How to Play

  1. Decide how many rounds you want to play.
  2. Choose between Rock, Paper, or Scissor when prompted.
  3. The computer will randomly choose its move.
  4. The winner of each round is determined by the rules:
    • Rock beats Scissor
    • Scissor beats Paper
    • Paper beats Rock
  5. The player with the most points after all rounds wins the game!
